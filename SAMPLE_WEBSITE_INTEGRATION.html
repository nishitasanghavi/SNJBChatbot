About SNJB

SNJB's Late Sau. Kantabai Bhavarlalji Jain College of Engineering is a premier institution located in Chandwad, Nashik. Established in 2004, we are committed to providing quality engineering education.

With NAAC 'A+' accreditation and NBA recognition, we offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines.

Courses Offered

B.Tech Programs

Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Computer Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering

M.Tech Programs

Advanced specializations in Computer Engineering and Mechanical Engineering

MBA Program

Quality management education with industry focus

Admissions

We welcome talented students from across the country. Our admission process is transparent and merit-based.

B.Tech Eligibility: 12th pass with Physics, Mathematics, and one science subject with minimum 45% marks.

MBA Eligibility: Bachelor's degree with valid entrance score (MAH-MBA-CET, CAT, CMAT, etc.)
